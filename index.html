Dr. Fan Zhang is a faculty member at Harvard Medical School (HMS) and Brigham and Women's Hospital (BWH), Boston. He received his PhD in Computer Science at The University of Sydney, Australia, in 2016. His research interests focus on developing novel computational techniques for medical image analysis. He has published over 90 peer-reviewed papers in leading international journals and proceedings of top international conferences such as NeuroImage, MedIA, IEEE TMI, MICCAI and CVPR. His work is fundamentally interdisciplinary and is presented in Medical Image Computing, dMRI Tractography&Atlasing, and Clinical&Research Applications.
