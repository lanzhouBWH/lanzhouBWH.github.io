Overview

Zhou Lan is a faculty biostatistician at the Center for Clinical Investigation (CCI) within the Channing Division of Network Medicine at Brigham and Women's Hospital. Zhou collaborates closely with investigators in the Department of Radiology (0.6 FTE). He earned his Ph.D. in Statistics from North Carolina State University and received the Paige Plagge Award. His research focuses on statistical methodologies for MRI data, spatial statistics, Bayesian computing, and longitudinal data analysis. His work appears in journals including JRSS, JAMIA Open, Biometrics, Bioinformatics, and more.
